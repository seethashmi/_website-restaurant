Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours?

We are open Monday to Sunday from 11:00 AM to 9:30 PM

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we offer both takeout and delivery options. You can place an order online or over the phone.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering services for events and special occasions. Please contact us for more details.

Do you have gluten-free options?

While our menu is primarily vegetarian, we do have some gluten-free options. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions.
